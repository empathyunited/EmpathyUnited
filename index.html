Mission

Empathy United transforms empathy from a feeling into coordinated action — mobilizing resources, raising awareness, and supporting humanitarian aid to civilians in Gaza while remaining steadfast in our commitment to assist any community facing crisis anywhere in the world.

What we do

We keep it practical: mobilize support, partner responsibly, and report clearly.

Mobilize resources

Grassroots fundraising and community coordination to meet urgent humanitarian needs.

Partner with vetted orgs

Work with reputable humanitarian organizations so aid reaches civilians effectively and safely.

Raise awareness

Education and responsible communication that keeps focus on human impact and help.

Measure outcomes

Regular updates that show how funds are allocated and what they accomplish.

Current initiative: Gaza humanitarian relief

Our immediate focus is supporting civilians facing urgent humanitarian crisis. We prioritize aid that addresses basic survival and dignity — and coordinate with organizations already operating on the ground.

  • Emergency food access
  • Clean water and essential supplies
  • Medical support and critical resources
  • Ongoing updates and transparent reporting

Transparency & accountability

Trust is built through clarity. We publish where funds go, why decisions are made, and what outcomes we can verify.

  • Partner details and rationale (as appropriate and safe)
  • Regular initiative updates and progress summaries
  • Public contact point for questions and collaboration

How we operate (early-stage)

  • Humanitarian framing: civilians and urgent need
  • Public reporting habits from day one
  • Formal nonprofit structure to follow
