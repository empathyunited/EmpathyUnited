Mission

Empathy United transforms empathy from a feeling into coordinated action — mobilizing resources, raising awareness, and supporting humanitarian relief for civilians in Gaza, while remaining steadfast in our commitment to help anyone facing urgent need anywhere.

What We Do

Mobilize resources

Grassroots fundraising and community coordination to meet urgent humanitarian needs.

Partner responsibly

We work with vetted humanitarian organizations so aid reaches civilians effectively and safely.

Raise awareness

Responsible messaging that keeps focus on people and practical help — not spectacle.

Report clearly

Regular updates showing allocations, partner activity, and outcomes we can verify.

Transparency

What you can expect

  • Clear breakdowns of funds raised and allocations
  • Partner notes and rationale where appropriate and safe
  • Regular updates and progress summaries
  • A public contact point for questions and collaboration

How we operate

  • Vetting-first approach to partnerships
  • Humanitarian framing centered on civilians and urgent need
  • Public reporting habits from day one
  • Formal nonprofit structure to follow
